About SofaLux

Crafting comfort since 2010, we blend timeless design with modern luxury — because every home deserves a story.

Our story

What started as a small workshop in Vermont is now a destination for those who appreciate understated luxury. We partner with master artisans who use time‑honored techniques and sustainably sourced materials. Every SofaLux piece carries a legacy of comfort and intention.

Why we’re different

Sustainable materials

We source reclaimed wood, non‑toxic finishes, and certified fabrics that age beautifully.

Artisan craftsmanship

Each frame is carved, stitched, and assembled by hands that have honed their skill for decades.

Lifetime comfort

We design for heirloom status — cushions that keep their shape, joinery that lasts generations.
